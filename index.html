Why Choose JMideal Multi Split Systems

Our systems offer numerous advantages over traditional single-split units, providing superior comfort and efficiency for your home or business.

55% Electricity Cost Reduction

Our advanced Inverter Quattro™ technology can reduce energy consumption by up to 55%, significantly lowering your electricity bills.

Up to 150% High Combination Rate

One outdoor unit can connect to multiple indoor units, providing flexible installation options and optimal capacity utilization.

Wide Operation Range

Our systems operate efficiently even in extreme temperatures, ensuring comfort all year round in West Africa's challenging climate.

Active Power Factor Correction

Our systems utilize up to 88-99% of the input power supply effectively, maximizing energy efficiency and reducing waste.

Cost-Effective Installation

Fewer outdoor units mean reduced installation costs, less space required, and a cleaner, more aesthetically pleasing appearance.

Individual Temperature Control

Each indoor unit can be controlled independently, allowing personalized comfort settings for different rooms or zones.

Multi Split vs. Single Split: The Clear Choice

When comparing multi-split systems to traditional single-split units, the advantages are clear. Our multi-split systems offer superior efficiency, flexibility, and cost savings.
